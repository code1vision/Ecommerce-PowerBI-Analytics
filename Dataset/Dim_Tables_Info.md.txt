# Dimension Tables Overview

This document describes the dimension tables used in the E-Commerce Power BI Analytics project.
All dimension tables are designed to support a star schema model and enable efficient slicing,
filtering, and aggregation of fact data.

---

## 1. DimDate

**Purpose:**  
Provides a unified calendar table for time-based analysis and time intelligence calculations.

**Key Columns:**
- Date – Calendar date (primary date field)
- Year – Year number
- Quarter – Quarter (Q1–Q4)
- Month – Month number
- Month Name – Month name
- Day – Day of month
- Day of Week – Day name/number
- IsWeekendCustom – Weekend indicator

**Usage:**
- Revenue trends
- YoY, YTD, MTD calculations
- Seasonal analysis

---

## 2. DimChannel

**Purpose:**  
Represents the sales and marketing channels through which orders are placed.

**Key Columns:**
- Channel – Channel name (Web, App, Marketplace, Affiliate, Social)

**Usage:**
- Channel-wise revenue and profit analysis
- Marketing performance comparison
- Customer acquisition analysis

---

## 3. DimRegion

**Purpose:**  
Represents the geographical regions where orders are fulfilled.

**Key Columns:**
- Region – Region name (India, APAC, EMEA, AMER)

**Usage:**
- Regional performance analysis
- SLA and logistics comparison
- Shipping cost analysis

---

## 4. DimCustomer

**Purpose:**  
Represents unique customers in the system.

**Key Columns:**
- CustomerID – Unique customer identifier

**Usage:**
- Customer acquisition metrics
- Order count analysis
- Customer behavior insights

---

## Data Modeling Notes

- All dimension tables follow a star schema design.
- Relationships are single-directional from dimensions to fact tables.
- Dimension tables are used for slicing, filtering, and grouping measures.
- No calculated columns are used for KPIs; all business logic is handled through DAX measures.

---

## Disclaimer

All datasets used in this project are synthetic and created for learning and demonstration purposes only.
